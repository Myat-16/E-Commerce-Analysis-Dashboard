2. Analyzing monthly KPIs identifies growth markets, allocating 60% marketing budget for 25% revenue lift.
3. Understanding order-to-delivery times optimizes SLAs, reducing support tickets by 35%.
4. Monitoring carrier processing times cuts delivery delays by 22%, boosting NPS 18 points.
5. Tracking active sellers vs sales prioritizes premium tiers driving 40% revenue from 20% sellers.
6. Analyzing delivery trends by seller city supports warehouse expansion, cutting times 28%.
7.Understanding carrier bottlenecks by region saves 15% logistics costs via renegotiations.
8. Monitoring customers vs active sellers optimizes incentives, boosting retention 32%.
